## Magnifier to Launch Settings Menu

This is a simple initial escape, using Magnifier to escape to Settings.  

 - Press the Windows and "+" key to activate Magnifier 
 - Click the Gearbox icon to expand the Speed/Voice dialog box
 - Click "Go to Settings"

Once Settings opens you can explore settings or you can move on to:

 - [Escape to Less Restrictive Browser](https://github.com/CroodSolutions/CTRL-ESC-HOST/blob/main/2%20-%20Kiosk%20Playbook/1%20-%20Win11%20Kiosk%20Mode%20-%20Attended%20Access/2%20-%20Next%20Steps/Escape%20to%20Less%20Restrictive%20Browser.md)
 - Navigate to System > Display > Graphics and click "Add desktop app" to expose [File Open Dialog](https://github.com/CroodSolutions/CTRL-ESC-HOST/blob/main/2%20-%20Kiosk%20Playbook/1%20-%20Win11%20Kiosk%20Mode%20-%20Attended%20Access/2%20-%20Next%20Steps/Launch%20Terminal%20from%20File%20Open%20Dialog%20Box%20via%20Preview%20Pane.md)

Remember: Only test on your own kiosks and/or with proper written permission and following all appropriate laws and industry ethics / best practices
